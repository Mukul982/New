How to Add Products

  1. Go to your Teespring dashboard and copy the product link
  2. Paste the link in the "Product Link" field below
  3. Optionally add a title, price, and description
  4. Click "Add Product" to add it to your store
  5. Switch to the "Store Front" tab to see your products
